Artificial Intelligence for Conversational Robo-Advisor
Min-Yuh Day,Jian-Ting Lin, YuanChih Chen
IEEE/ACM (Slides Presentation)

| abstract | pdf |

With the upcoming of the AI era, AI combines withFintech (Financial Technology; FinTech) has become a development tendency in the financial industry. However,the literatures are not as common investigated into deep learning about the application of automated financialmanagement. This research focuses on the application of Fintech and deep learning in asset allocation, and expectsto optimize the investment portfolio. The best investing in portfolio Index-based funds based on Taiwan's index-typesecurities investment trust funds (ETFs) are the main investment targets, and the time series related models fordeep learning – Long Short Term Memory predicts the increase of each investment target, and finds the bestinvestment portfolio in combination with the relevant asset allocation theory; the HCI dialogue service adopts AIMLand a Generative model. The AIML achieves the effect of dialogue by making a knowledge, and the generated modelis completed by the Sequence To Sequence model. In this research, we use the Markowitz Mean-Variance Model andBlack-Litterment as our allocation theories, the result showing that the Blacklitterment model had a betteraccumulated return performance and outperformed other strategies.
